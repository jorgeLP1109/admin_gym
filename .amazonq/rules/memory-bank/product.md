# Product Overview

## Project Purpose
LEVEL UP S&A CENTER is a comprehensive administrative system designed for gym management. It provides complete control over students, classes, instructors, payments, and accounting operations for fitness centers.

## Value Proposition
- Centralized management of all gym operations in a single platform
- Automated payment tracking with flexible billing cycles (biweekly/monthly)
- Medical records integration for student safety
- Real-time financial reporting and accounting
- Streamlined enrollment and class scheduling

## Key Features

### Student Management
- Complete student profiles with personal information
- Medical records (blood type, allergies, conditions, medications)
- Emergency contact information
- Minor student support with legal guardian registration
- Photo upload capability
- Active/inactive status tracking

### Instructor Management
- Professor profiles with specialization tracking
- Salary and contract date management
- Assignment to multiple classes
- Performance and availability tracking

### Class Management
- Flexible class scheduling with JSON-based timetables
- Capacity limits and enrollment tracking
- Dual pricing structure (monthly/biweekly)
- Class descriptions and requirements
- Instructor assignment

### Enrollment System
- Student-to-class registration
- Payment modality selection (monthly/biweekly)
- Custom payment day configuration (1-31)
- Enrollment history tracking
- Automatic conflict prevention

### Payment Control
- Automated payment tracking per enrollment
- Due date management
- Multiple payment methods support
- Payment references and notes
- Historical payment records

### Accounting Module
- Income and expense tracking
- Category-based transaction organization
- Payment method recording
- Reference and receipt management
- Date-based financial reporting
- Automatic income registration from student payments

### Reporting & Analytics
- Financial summaries (income/expenses)
- Student enrollment statistics
- Payment status monitoring
- Class occupancy reports
- Custom date range queries

## Target Users

### Primary Users
- Gym administrators and owners
- Front desk staff
- Accounting personnel

### Use Cases
- Daily check-in and enrollment management
- Payment collection and tracking
- Financial reporting for business decisions
- Student medical information access for emergencies
- Class scheduling and capacity planning
- Instructor workload management

## Business Model
- Internal management system for LEVEL UP S&A CENTER
- Supports both physical and administrative operations
- Designed for single-location gym with potential for multi-location expansion
